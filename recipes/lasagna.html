Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melty cheese. It's the ultimate comfort food!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Sauté beef, onion, and garlic in a pan until browned. Add sauce and simmer.
  4. In a baking dish, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and repeat.
  5. Top with Parmesan and bake for 25–30 minutes until bubbly and golden.
