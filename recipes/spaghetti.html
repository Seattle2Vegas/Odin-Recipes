Spaghetti

A classic italian dish with tomato sauce andnoodles

Ingredients

  • spaghetti noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • ground beef
  • garlic, oniom, salt, pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

    • Steps

    1. Boil water in a large pot and cook spaghetti noodles according to package instructions.
    2. In a separate pan, brown ground beef with chopped garlic and onion until fully cooked.
    3. Add tomato sauce to the beef mixture and simmer for 10 minutes.
    4. Drain spaghetti and combine with the sauce.
    5. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
